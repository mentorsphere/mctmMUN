MCTM MUN

Welcome to MCTM MUN 2025 We are proud to announce the inaugural edition of MCTM Model United Nations (MCTM MUN)! Hosted by MCTM Chidambaram Chettyar Matriculation School, this first-ever MUN conference brings together passionate students from across Chennai to engage in meaningful dialogue, debate global issues, and develop real-world diplomatic skills. Join us for a transformative experience that blends leadership, learning, and collaboration.
