MCTM MUN

Welcome to MCTM MUN 2025! We are delighted to unveil the first-ever edition of the MCTM Model United Nations! Hosted by MCTM Chidambaram Chettyar International School, this first-ever MUN conference brings together passionate students from across Chennai to engage in meaningful dialogue, debate global issues, and develop real-world diplomatic skills. Join us for a transformative experience that blends leadership, learning, and collaboration.

Founded in 2009, M.Ct.M. Chidambaram Chettyar International School in Chennai offers globally acclaimed IB and Cambridge curriculum. It fosters independent learning and equips students for higher education worldwide. The school, located on a spacious Mylapore campus with modern facilities, is backed by the M.Ct.M. Foundation. It honors M.Ct.M. Chidambaram Chettyar, a visionary leader, philanthropist, and parliamentarian known for his business acumen and commitment to education. His legacy of excellence and progress lives on through the school's mission to cultivate self-sufficient, confident learners prepared to face global challenges.

Letter to Delegates

Dear Delegates, With great enthusiasm, I invite you to participate in the inaugural edition of MCTM MUN 2025, the first-ever Model United Nations conference hosted by M.Ct.M Chidambaram Chettyar International School, scheduled to take place from July 25th to 26th, 2025.

As the Principal of M.Ct.M Chidambaram Chettyar International School, it is my honor to welcome young leaders from around the world to our campus and provide a distinguished platform for engaging in meaningful dialogue, exchanging diverse perspectives, and cultivating the essential skills for future leadership.

Our mission is to inspire and empower young leaders through experiential learning in international diplomacy, fostering critical thinking, collaborative problem-solving, and effective communication. Guided by our vision to cultivate globally conscious citizens who engage thoughtfully with pressing world issues—upholding empathy, integrity, and data-driven decision-making—we have prepared concise background materials on topics such as sustainable development, mitigating terrorism, global economic recovery, and arms control to support your research and debate.

MCTM MUN 2025 offers dynamic committee sessions, a platform to sharpen your negotiation skills, and countless opportunities to build meaningful connections with fellow delegates beyond formal debate. Whether you're a veteran of the MUN circuit or attending your first conference, you'll encounter both stimulating challenges and a strong sense of fellowship as you collaborate to craft resolutions rooted in mutual understanding and creative problem-solving.

Best Regards,

Ms. Sangita Varma
Head of the International School
