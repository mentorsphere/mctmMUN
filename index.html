MCTM MUN

Welcome to MCTM MUN 2025 We are proud to announce the inaugural edition of MCTM Model United Nations (MCTM MUN)! Hosted by MCTM Chidambaram Chettyar Matriculation School, this first-ever MUN conference brings together passionate students from across Chennai to engage in meaningful dialogue, debate global issues, and develop real-world diplomatic skills. Join us for a transformative experience that blends leadership, learning, and collaboration.

Founded in 2009, M.Ct.M. Chidambaram Chettyar International School in Chennai offers globally acclaimed IB and Cambridge curriculum. It fosters independent learning and equips students for higher education worldwide. The school, located on a spacious Mylapore campus with modern facilities, is backed by the M.Ct.M. Foundation. It honors M.Ct.M. Chidambaram Chettyar, a visionary leader, philanthropist, and parliamentarian known for his business acumen and commitment to education. His legacy of excellence and progress lives on through the school's mission to cultivate self-sufficient, confident learners prepared to face global challenges.

Letter to Delegates

Dear Delegates, With great enthusiasm, I invite you to participate in the inaugural edition of MCTM MUN 2025, the first-ever Model United Nations conference hosted by M.Ct.M Chidambaram Chettyar School, scheduled to take place from July 25th to 26th, 2025. As the Principal of M.Ct.M Chidambaram Chettyar School, it is my honor to welcome young leaders from around the world to our campus and provide a distinguished platform for engaging in meaningful dialogue, exchanging diverse perspectives, and cultivating the essential skills for future leadership. Our mission is to inspire and empower young leaders through experiential learning in international diplomacy, fostering critical thinking, collaborative problem-solving, and effective communication. Guided by our vision to cultivate globally conscious citizens who engage thoughtfully with pressing world issues—upholding empathy, integrity, and data-driven decision-making—we have prepared concise background materials on topics such as sustainable development, mitigating terrorism, global economic recovery, and arms control to support your research and debate. MCTM MUN 2025 promises lively committee sessions, opportunities to refine your negotiation techniques, and moments to connect with fellow delegates beyond the dais. Whether you are a seasoned MUN participant or new to the conference circuit, you will find both challenge and camaraderie as you work together to draft resolutions that reflect shared understanding and innovative solutions. Best Regards, Ms. Sangita Varma Head of the International School
